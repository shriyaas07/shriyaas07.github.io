Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis

A business analytics project evaluating marketing campaign effectiveness across channels using performance metrics and dashboard-driven insights.

Business Objective

To analyze marketing performance, identify high-performing channels, optimize customer acquisition cost, and improve overall campaign ROI.

Metrics Analyzed

  • Return on Investment (ROI)
  • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
  • Channel-wise performance
  • Campaign conversion trends
