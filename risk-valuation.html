Financial Performance, Risk & Valuation Analytics

An end-to-end financial intelligence system built using Python and Power BI to evaluate financial performance, risk profile, cash flow sustainability, and intrinsic valuation of a large listed company.

Project Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw financial statements into decision-ready business intelligence by analyzing profitability, financial risk, cash flows, growth sustainability, and intrinsic value using DCF.

Analytical Coverage

  • Multi-year financial performance analysis
  • Profitability and margin analytics
  • Capital structure and financial risk assessment
  • Cash flow analysis and forecasting
  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) valuation

Interactive Power BI Dashboard

The dashboard provides an interactive view of financial health, profitability, risk exposure, cash flows, and valuation insights.

Key Business Insights

  • Revenue and cash flows show stable multi-year growth
  • Profit margins indicate improving operational efficiency
  • Debt remains controlled relative to equity
  • Cash flows support earnings quality
  • DCF valuation provides an objective estimate of intrinsic value

Tools & Technologies

Python (Pandas, NumPy), Jupyter Notebook, Power BI, Financial Modeling, DCF
